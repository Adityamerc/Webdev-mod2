Our Menu

Dosa

A dosa is a rice pancake, originating from South India, made from a fermented batter.Its main ingredients are rice and black gram, ground together in a fine, smooth batter with a dash of salt.

Puri

Puri is a deep-fat fried bread made from unleavened whole-wheat flour that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is eaten for breakfast or as a snack or light meal.

Paneer Masala

Paneer butter masala is an Indian dish of marinated paneer cheese served in a spiced gravy. It is a vegetarian alternative to chicken tikka masala.
